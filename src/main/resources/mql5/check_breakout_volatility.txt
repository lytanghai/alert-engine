🔥 How Breakout Detection Works

📊 What Happens Internally

Let’s say market was ranging.

Suddenly:
Candle becomes large
ATR jumps
Volume doubles
Price closes above last 10 candles high

→ That means strong buyers entered
→ Real breakout
→ Telegram alert sent

Same logic for sell side.

1️⃣ Volatility Expansion
Current ATR > Previous ATR × Multiplier  === Means market just became active.

2️⃣ Volume Spike  
Current Volume > Previous Volume × Multiplier === Means real participation (not fake move).

3️⃣ Breakout Confirmation === This avoids fake signals inside range.
In case of breaking up:
Current Close > Highest High of last 10 candles
In case of break down:
Current Close < Lowest Low of last 10 candles
